BVC Sport Club Welcomes You!

Welcome to the annual SPORTING EVENT hosted by the BVC Sport Club. No need for any accessories just bring yourself or feel free to invite a friend.

Who Are We?

WE are the BVC Sport Club. We are responsible for hosting fun sporting activities and games. Our aim is to keep you active, happy, healthy, and to just see you have fun.

What is this event about?

This event involves a collection of mini sporting activities and games that you can play or watch professional athletes play. There will be snacks and drinks available at every booth you visit. So bring yourself and a friend and let us have fun!

Registration

Registration is super easy, just fill out a form, but remember your status in college:
  • For Students: $10
  • For Teachers: $50
  • For Volunteers: No Fee
